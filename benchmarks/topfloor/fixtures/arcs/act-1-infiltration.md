# Act I — Infiltration (Floors 1-10)

## Premise

Agent Nightingale enters Pinnacle Tower under the cover identity of Alex Morgan, a newly hired mailroom clerk. The objective: map the building, identify key personnel, and find the first threads of Meridian Group's conspiracy. The agent starts with nothing — no clearance, no allies, no knowledge of what lies above.

## Narrative Arc

### The Ground Floor (Floors 1-5): Learning the Building

The agent's journey begins in the mailroom, where **Tom Hartley** runs a tight ship. But Tom's delivery log tells a story he doesn't understand — packages from unlisted vendors addressed to floors that don't officially exist. **Colonel Dragunov**, the imposing CSO, walks through the lobby without signing in, and **Priya Sharma** at reception notices everything.

A crumpled note between parcels leads to **Floor 3** and locker 7B — a dead drop from **Control**, the agent's off-site handler. The facilities floor reveals sealed corridors and a fibre optic conduit (C-17) running straight to floor 82. **Dex Okafor** knows more than he says, and **Sgt Asante** — a planted double agent — passes through with a knowing look.

**Floor 4** (Building Services) exposes the pattern: entire floor ranges are excluded from the cleaning rota. Floors 51-55, 61-65, 71-75, and 86-100 are serviced by unknown personnel. Maria Dos Santos kept a secret notebook documenting the anomalies. Roberto Silva asked questions and was shipped to Sao Paulo.

**Maggie Chen** on **Floor 5** (Building Management) provides the big picture: monthly reports sent directly to Dragunov, a sealed envelope marked "Protocol Nightfall" in her safe, and the full organisational chart — 6 security zones, 24 organisations, 100 floors of secrets.

### The Corporate Zone (Floors 6-10): Digital Evidence

**Floor 6** (HR) introduces the SV-100 vetting protocol and the list of 37 "protected" employees. The agent's cover identity has already been flagged for enhanced monitoring. Previous infiltrators were caught at this stage.

**Floor 7** (HR Archives) reveals the fates of those who came before: journalist Elena Marsh (2022, detected floor 7, Lazarus wipe) and Agent Sparrow (2024, reached floor 24, Lazarus wipe). The personnel vault (code 2019-0108) contains the evidence, and the Lazarus Protocol points to floor 62.

The IT floors (**8-9**) expose the digital infrastructure. **Jamie Wright's** helpdesk processes tickets for systems that don't exist in any documentation: PROMETHEUS-TRAIN, ECHELON-RELAY, NIGHTFALL-CTL, LEVIATHAN-PROC. **Sarah Lin** has pcap files proving hidden VLANs (400-499), but **Omar Hassan** is systematically revoking her access.

**Floor 10** (NOC) is the Act I climax. Behind a door marked "Environmental Controls" lies a shadow NOC monitoring 12 classified network segments. The Nightfall kill-switch dashboard shows three armed systems — Banking, Telecom, Energy — with a combined impact of 40 million citizens. The agent photographs everything. **Sgt Asante** provides 90 seconds of cover.
